Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some questions you may have about the class

Sign Up

How does DACC help my future?

DACC helps students figure out a path into a career they are interested in. You get to experience projects in class that you would actually be doing on the job.

How will transportation look for those who need it?

Your parents will need to contact your home school in order for you to have bus transportation from the home school to DACC. The bus will also be here if a ride home is needed.

What certifications will we receive from this program?

You will receive a total of 7 certifications during the program and they are extremely useful for employment.
